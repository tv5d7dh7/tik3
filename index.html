About this app

TikTok 18+ is an unofficial, third-party developed modified version of the app that offers all the features available in TikTok, but it is designed for a mature audience. Users must be 18 years old to access restricted content. Unlike standard social media apps, there is no need to enter personal details to log in or sign up; you can start watching short videos without creating an account.
